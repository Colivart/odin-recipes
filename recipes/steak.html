Perfect Steak at Home

Description

Ingredients

  • 1.5" think Ribeye
  • 1 pat butter
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • Cast Iron Pan

Instructions

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper
  2. Heat pan to high heat
  3. Add steak and sear, flipping every 45 seconds
  4. Cook steak to ~5-10 degrees below target temp
  5. Add butter and garlic cloves, baste steak while bringing steak up to temp
  6. Remove from heat once done, cover in aluminum foil for 8 mins to rest
  7. Enjoy the best steak you've ever cooked
