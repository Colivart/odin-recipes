5 Alarm Chili

Description

Ingredients

  • 28 oz Can of Tomatos
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 10 oz guajilo chilis
  • can of chipotle en adobo sauce
  • 2lbs onions
  • 8 jalepenos
  • 12 medium garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 cup beer
  • seasonings

Instructions

  1. Soak chilis in hot water for 45 minutes
  2. Dice onions, jalepenos and garlic
  3. Blend chilis and tomatoes together
  4. Cook ground beef
  5. Sautee onions and jalepneos till slightly browned
  6. add seasonings and garlic to onions, toss till fragrant
  7. add all ingredients, simmer for 1 hr
